About this Project

This project serves a guide on how the internet functions. There are many intricacies that seem complicated on the surface, but by reading the various topics on this site, you will be able to understand the underlying technologies of the internet and why they are so important. By watching the various videos and developing this site I gained a much better understanding of how data is transferred so quickly and efficiently. I also got more time to play around with and get more comfortable with html and bootstrapping
