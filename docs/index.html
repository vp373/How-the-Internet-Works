How the Internet Works

What is the Internet?

The internet is a collection of computers that communicate with each other through a communications network. Humans are increasingly dependant on it and often take it for granted. It enables us to communicate with each other no matter where we are.

Watch this video to get a better idea of what exactly the internet is and how it started!

How Does Data Travel?

Computers communicate with each other in bits. Essentially these are just a series of 1s and 0s. Today, we transfer bits through either electricity, light, or radio waves. There are many factors which determine the speed of the data transfer including bandwidth and latency.
